Who Are Accredited Music Therapists?

To achieve accreditation from the Canadian Association of Music Therapy (CAMT), music therapists must:

  • Have a university degree in Music Therapy,

  • Complete a 1000-hour supervised clinical internship, and

  • Submit a written portfolio for approval by the Accreditation Review Board

On approval, candidates receive the “Music Therapist Accredited” (MTA) title. The CAMT requires ongoing education for all MTA holders in order to maintain accreditation. MTA practitioners are therefore constantly seeking advancements to the theoretical and practical use of music therapy techniques.
