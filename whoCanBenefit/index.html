Who Can Benefit From Music Therapy?

Music therapy has proven successful for individuals of all ages with a wide range of abilities and musical backgrounds. Music therapists are trained to help clients overcome any sort of physical, mental or emotional challenge, but the best way to know whether or not music therapy is suitable for your needs is to try it.

Music therapists typically work out of the the following settings:

  • Medical hospitals
  • Psychiatric hospitals
  • Rehabilitative facilities
  • Agencies servicing individuals with special needs
  • Community mental health centers
  • Drug and alcohol programs
  • Senior and long-term care centres
  • Hospice programs
  • Child care programs and schools
  • Private practice
