About

Musictherapyservice.ca is owned and maintained by Nancy Leung.

Nancy graduated from Wilfrid Laurier University in 2010 with Honours Bachelor of Music Therapy degree and completed her internship at various multi-ethnic long-term-care facilities in Toronto, ON. She has worked closely with individuals of all ages with various physical, emotional and mental health issues. Her specialized interest includes working with individuals in post-stroke speech and physical rehabilitation. She is a classically trained pianist with an ARCT diploma in performance and uses the guitar and voice in her clinical practice.

She is passionate about working with individuals to enhance their own quality of life and actively volunteers with L’Arche Toronto and Philip Aziz Centre for hospice care. She recently completed training in Neurologic Music Therapy (NMT) and is currently learning to apply Diane Austin’s vocal psychotherapy model and techniques as she attends the advanced certificate program offered by the Vancouver Vocal Psychotherapy Institute.

Nancy is the Vice President of the Music Therapy Association of Ontario (MTAO).
