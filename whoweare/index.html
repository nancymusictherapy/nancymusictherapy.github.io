About

MusicTherapyService.ca is run by Nancy Leung. Nancy has worked closely with clients of all ages with various physical, emotional, and mental health issues. She specializes in post-stroke speech rehabilitation and works with children to develop communication and social skills. She currently attends the Advanced Vocal Psychotherapy Certificate program. Nancy has also recently completed training in Neurological Music Therapy (NMT), held at the University of Toronto.

As vice-president of the Music Therapy Association of Ontario, Nancy is actively involved with developing and supporting the profession within the province. She has presented in numerous workshops for parents and healthcare professionals to promote the benefits and use of music therapy.

Nancy graduated from Wilfrid Laurier University in 2010 with an Honours Bachelor of Music Therapy degree, then completed her internship at various multi-ethnic long-term care facilities in Toronto. She is a classically-trained pianist with an ARCT Diploma in Performance. She uses guitar and voice in her clinical practice. Nancy is passionate about working with individuals to enhance their quality of life and actively volunteers with L’Arche Toronto and Philip Aziz Centre for hospice care.
