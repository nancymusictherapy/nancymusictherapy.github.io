Our Services

We offer customized in-home and in-facility music therapy programs, servicing Hamilton, Ontario.

This includes:

  • Group and/or individual music therapy sessions
  • Music therapy for private clients
  • Music therapy for seniors facilities
  • Adaptive music lessons
  • Inservices and workshops for healthcare and education professionals

Piano lessons and R.C.M. examination preparation are also available.
