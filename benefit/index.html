Who Can Benefit From Music Therapy?

Music therapy has proven successful for individuals with a wide range of ages, abilities, and musical backgrounds. Although music therapists are trained to help clients overcome any sort of physical, mental or emotional challenge, the best way to learn whether music therapy is a good match is to try it.

Music therapists can be found, but are not limited to working in the following settings:

  • Medical hospitals
  • Psychiatric hospitals
  • Rehabilitative facilities
  • Agencies servicing individuals with special needs
  • Community mental health centers
  • Drug and alcohol programs
  • Senior and long-term care centres
  • Hospice programs
  • Child care programs and schools
  • Private practice
